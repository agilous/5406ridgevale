This custom built home is truly one of a kind as designed by noted architect Paul Studer. The near 7000 square feet of living area throughout both levels offer superior amenities in near perfect condition. The main floor offers a formal entry hall, impressive living room with gas fireplace, library/office, complete gourmet kitchen, formal dining room, and rightly positioned breakfast area. A master suite is complete with gas fireplace and beautifully complimented by a master bath complete with heated floors, whirlpool tub, and closets galore. Two other bedrooms have their own accompanying bath facilities. A mere sampling of the lower level accruements would include two guest bedrooms with baths, walk in cedar closet, media center, commercial food service facilities, plus craft and game rooms. The living and entertaining conveniences of this home are superior in all instances.
